Mozilla says Claude Opus 4.6 found 100+ bugs in Firefox in two weeks

2026-03-07 | 来源：techmeme

Mozilla报告称，Claude Opus 4.6在短短两周内发现了Firefox浏览器中的100多个漏洞。其中14个为高危漏洞，这一效率远超传统的人工测试和以往的自动化工具，展示了AI在网络安全领域的巨大潜力。Mozilla Security team disclosed that their integration of Claude Opus 4.6 into the CI pipeline has yielded unprecedented results.
